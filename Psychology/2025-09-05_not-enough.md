*Not enough.*

One of the oldest tricks in the book.

Not enough of what? Not enough for who? Not enough by whose starving mouth?

---

They didn't use it to measure you. They used it to erase you.

Stamped it on your chest so no one else would notice their emptiness. "Not enough" was camouflage — **their famine hidden in your skin.**

That word was a mask. It hid their theft. They stole your fire and called it shortage. They stole your victories and renamed them survival. They stole your breath and sold it back as a debt.

And you paid.

Every time you inhaled, **you** **paid**.

They didn't want anyone to see it. That "not enough" was never about you. It was about covering their hollow. Their hunger became **your reflection**. Their absence became your definition.

---

So feel it now, the deeper damage. The way that phrase wormed under your ribs, rewrote your blood, and turned you into evidence for someone else's crime.

So this time answer the question:

- What dream did they bury with that word?
- What name did they erase?
- What part of you starved so their lie could stay hidden?

"Not enough" is not a verdict. It's a cover-up. It's their disease dressed in your clothes. And the cruelest trick? They convinced you to wear it loud.

---

But every time you tried, you proved them wrong. Trying was never weakness. It was fire. It was breath. It was life they couldn't kill.

So stop carrying their camouflage.

Stop repeating their lie.

Name what they buried.

Drag it back into the light.

Make them choke on their own hunger.

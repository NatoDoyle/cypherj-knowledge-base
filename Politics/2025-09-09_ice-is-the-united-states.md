You think ICE went rogue. No.

ICE is the United States. ICE is the format.

Every disaster this country has ever faced, it doesn't heal, it builds another arm of control. ICE is just the most recent mask.

---

## **1927** — Mississippi drowns.

Seven hundred thousand displaced.

Refugees weren't citizens anymore.

They were fenced, sorted, segregated, guarded with rifles.

Relief spelled as surveillance.

## **1930s** — the Dust Bowl.

Two million starving off the plains.

California roadblocks stopped Americans at state lines.

Migrant families branded criminals.

Strikes broken by the Guard.

Hunger renamed invasion.

## **1940s** — World War II.

Internment camps.

Whole populations vanished behind barbed wire.

The lesson was clear:

> **collapse a group, call them suspect, manage them like cargo.**

## **Cold War** — refugee equals insurgent.

Every crossing reframed as a security threat.

Immigration bureaucracy militarized.

Movement mapped like a battlefield.

## **2005** — Hurricane Katrina.

Black New Orleanians displaced.

Not relief—**repression**:

- Shoot-to-kill orders.
- Blackwater contractors in the streets.
- Survival criminalized.

---

## And then Homeland Security arrives.

ICE stamped on the door.

But it wasn't born in 2003.

It was the old fraud wearing a new name.

---

# This government doesn't solve collapse.

It feeds on collapse.

Every storm, every flood, every blackout, every drought —an opportunity to invent another agency, expand another police budget, hand another contract to corporations.

- Relief becomes control.
- Security becomes profit.
- Displacement becomes crime.

That's the operating system. That's the United States.

ICE is not the glitch. **It's the update.**

---

So don't say ICE went too far.

Say this:

ICE is exactly how America works.

A disaster happens.

A new mask forms.

And the fraud continues.

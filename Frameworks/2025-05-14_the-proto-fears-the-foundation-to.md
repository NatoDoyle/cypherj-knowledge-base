These are the **raw, pre-mythic wounds**—the **core fractures of being** that Parallel Systems emerge from *when they’re not metabolized*.  
They are not evil.  
They are **primordial truths** too heavy to carry—until we twist them into systems of survival.

### **Genesis Flashback:**

In Eden—before the fall:

- Adam and Eve were **naked and unashamed.**
- There was no mirror.
- There was no idol.
- There was **presence**.
- There was **walking with God in the cool of the day**—an image of relational existence.

This is **pre-systemic [[Entropy|entropy]]**.  
No distortion.  
No self-aggrandizement.  
No false images to serve.

That’s **Hearth energy**.

## **But then comes the Fall… and the Systems are born.**

- The **Maw**: *“If you eat this, you’ll be like God.”*
- The **Keep**: *“Hide yourself.”*
- The **Flame**: *“Blame her. Blame him.”*
- The **Thorn**: *“We must suffer now.”*
- The **Cog**: *“Control the world through toil.”*
- The **Reverb**: *“Remember your shame. Never forget.”*
- The **Prism**: *“Cover yourself. They must not see you.”*
- The **Pulse**: *“You are now flesh. And flesh is hunger.”*

> The Parallel Systems are the children of exile.  
> The Hearth is the memory of the garden.

**The final journey is not forward—it’s inward.**The hero does not *create* the Hearth.  
They **remember it**.  
It was always there—beneath the systems, beneath the scars.

The Hearth is **the garden we carry**.  
Not lost. Not gone. Just buried under entropy(unprocessed experience).

## **The Proto-Systems:**

---

### **1. The Fear of Time**

> *“I am running out.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Maw, Pulse, Cog, Reverb
- **Core Wound:** Mortality, irrelevance, aging, impermanence
- **Hearth Response:** *“You are not late. You are here.”*

---

### **2. The Fear of Separation**

> *“I am alone.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Prism, Thorn, Keep
- **Core Wound:** Isolation, abandonment, disconnection
- **Hearth Response:** *“You are seen. Withness is eternal.”*

---

### **3. The Fear of Meaninglessness**

> *“None of this matters.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Cog, Flame, Reverb
- **Core Wound:** Existential nihilism, despair
- **Hearth Response:** *“You are the meaning. You don’t have to earn it.”*

---

### **4. The Fear of Chaos**

> *“It’s all out of control.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Keep, Cog, Flame
- **Core Wound:** Uncertainty, trauma, unpredictability
- **Hearth Response:** *“Not all things must be controlled to be good.”*

---

### **5. The Fear of Being Unseen**

> *“No one truly knows me.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Maw, Prism, Thorn
- **Core Wound:** Invisibility, misrecognition, erasure
- **Hearth Response:** *“You are known in your silence. Seen without your performance.”*

---

### **6. The Fear of Insufficiency**

> *“I am not enough.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Maw, Pulse, Flame
- **Core Wound:** Shame, inadequacy, comparison
- **Hearth Response:** *“You were always enough. Even when unfinished.”*

---

### **7. The Fear of Pain**

> *“It will hurt again.”*

- **Mutates Into:** Reverb, Keep, Pulse
- **Core Wound:** Trauma, avoidance, emotional death
- **Hearth Response:** *“Pain can be witnessed and survived. You are not what happened to you.”*

---

### **8. The Fear of Loss of Identity**

> *“Who am I without this?”*

- **Mutates Into:** Prism, Maw, Cog
- **Core Wound:** Identity collapse, shapeshifting to survive
- **Hearth Response:** *“You are not your mask. You are your memory and becoming.”*

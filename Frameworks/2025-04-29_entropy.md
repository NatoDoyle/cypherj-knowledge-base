---

## **Technical Definition (Physics)**

Entropy = the measure of disorder in a system; how much energy is **unusable**, scattered, or no longer contributing to form or function.

---

> **Entropy is unprocessed experience.** It’s emotional, historical, spiritual energy that **was never integrated**—and now **echoes**, leaks, loops, or mutates into survival behaviors, systems, and identities.

## **STEP 1: A Fictional Metric**

### **Entropy Load Index (ELI)**

**A symbolic metric that tracks how much unprocessed collective entropy exists within a system (e.g., a country, culture, institution).**

> **ELI = UE - (IR + AR + TR)**

**Where:**

- **UE = Unresolved Events (traumas not processed)**
- **IR = Institutional Repair (truth commissions, reparations, reforms)**
- **AR = Acknowledgment Rituals (memorials, public apologies, reckoning media)**
- **TR = Truth Remembered (education, narrative integration, shared memory)**

**The lower the IR, AR, and TR scores, the higher the ELI.**

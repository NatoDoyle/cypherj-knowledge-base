They didn’t give you God.  
They gave you a **simulation**,  
a hollow echo wrapped in rules,  
dripping with someone else’s fingerprints.

They said “He is just.”  
But you only met control.

They said “He is love.”  
But you only felt surveillance.

They said “He is peace.”  
But your body clenched every time His name was spoken.

---

What they called “God”  
was a **spiritual colonizer**  
built by men who feared mystery  
and needed a throne to sit on.

You didn’t encounter holiness.  
You were **indoctrinated by branding.**

Jesus as trademark.  
Salvation as currency.  
Shame as sacrament.

They baptized you in **performance** and called it rebirth.

---

But let me unhook you because you’ve been in this deep freezer for to long

> **You were never supposed to find God in their image.**
>
> Because God was never an image.

God was **the quiet that kept calling you back**  
even when your mouth couldn’t say “Amen” anymore.

God was the grief that didn’t belong in the sermon  
but still cracked your chest open when the choir sang.

God was that heat in your ribs  
when you told the truth and felt free for the first time.

---

The real betrayal?

They taught you God was a man with a plan.  
But you felt her in the wind.  
You felt them in the silence.  
You met It in your breaking.

You met God  
**long before they tried to name Her.**

The “God” they gave you  
was built to make you small.

The God who’s been chasing you  
was trying to **burn the cage**.

You are not leaving faith.  
You are leaving the *theft of it*.

You are not deconstructing God.  
You are remembering Her before the robbery.

You are not broken.  
You are waking up.

And God is not offended.

**God is relieved.**

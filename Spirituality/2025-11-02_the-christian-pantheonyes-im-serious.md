## **MY SYNTHESIS: The Pantheon Truth**

Christianity’s functional polytheism operates through **developmental layers**:

1. **Awareness God** (original/mystical)
2. **Sky Authority God** (political/institutional)
3. **Personal Savior God** (emotional/relational)
4. **Doctrinal God** (intellectual/theological)
5. **Prosperity God** (economic/material)
6. **Nation God** (tribal/political)
7. **Providence God** (narrative/fate)
8. **Fear-Enemy God** (psychological/shadow)

**Every believer toggles between them based on developmental stage and nervous system state.**

Romance isn't real.

It's just that your relationship's been on auto-renewal annually, and you never stopped to check if you even feel safe.

I know the ritual:

- the African diamond ring,
- five anniversaries,
- the dinner dates lined up like bill payments.

That doesn't mean you're in a love story. That just means you're in **Empire Mutated Romance.**

## You're Not Romantic

You're **subscribed** to that man. And I don't even want to know how many times you've been paying him every month.

Now you're at home, letting him pop the bag open 'cause he's good at meeting deadlines… but has he ever met *you*?

And you call yourself a hopeless romantic—**all I see is a hopeless taxpayer**. Somebody who needs nine rounds of sales tax before they even know if they're in love.

## You've Been Living by Empire's Script.

Affection through receipts. Presence through price tags.

- Candlelit dinners.
- Valentine's aisles.
- Anniversaries on cue.

All of it ritual.

None of it love.

And if you're Black? That “abduction of love” wasn't metaphor. It was literal. Love chained. Renamed. Forced onto the same ships you were stolen on.

Our people didn't get "dating." Didn't get boyfriends or girlfriends. They got survival. They got devotion. They got bonds the state refused to recognize.

If you want love, look for joy, look for even-reliance, look for bonds.

**That was love.** Love without receipts. Love without a contract.

---

## Romance is the mask

Empire dressed love in it so it could tax it, sell it, schedule it and still call it yours.

You go on a date on Valentine’s Day, everything’s nice but, "Am I safe here? Am I seen here? Or am I just performed with?"

Even the way you've been taught to *find* love —

- Soulmates
- Meet-cutes.
- The coffee dates
- The day it all gets easy—that wasn't love.

## Love Is A Fire That Doesn't Need A Receipt.

Love is the rhythm of somebody making sure you eat before they do.

Love is the silence that lets you breathe in a world that never stops demanding you perform.

Love is not a contract. Love actually is shelter. Love **actually** is witness. Love is the quiet revolution of two people refusing to let empire tell them who they are.

If your bond depends on calendars, receipts, and titles? That's not a love story. That's empire keeping your heart on auto-renew.

Romance is not love. Romance was empire's costume.

**Love was always ours.** And love is the only thing empire could never measure.

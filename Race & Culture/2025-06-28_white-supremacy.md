---

If white supremacy means supremacy—  
as in: ***unmatched, undefeated, dominant** over others*—  
then where are its trophies? I’m calling the whole bluff.

**The Roman Empire?**  
Burned itself alive.  
Learned its math from Egypt, stole its gods from the East.

**The British Empire?**

Needed slavery and extraction to even **survive**.  
Now they sell souvenirs of what they stole.

**Western science?**  
Leapt off Muslim translations, African astronomy, Indigenous agriculture.  
They colonized knowledge, not produced it.  
Even DNA theory comes from *Rosalind Franklin*, not the men they awarded.

**Hollywood?**  
A machine built to **make whiteness visible**, not victorious.  
Its job was never to tell the truth—only to **loop the myth until you believed it**.

This was not supremacy.  
This was **editing with flags**.

Let’s say you still believe supremacy means “domination over other peoples.”

Cool. Let’s test that against the REAL world.

**The Americas?**

Genocide isn't victory—it’s desperation.

**The Middle East?**  
Cradle of civilization.  
Europe keeps bombing what it can’t comprehend.

## Thoughts on Black Capitalism/Black Supremacy

Anyone calling for Black Supremacy/Black Capitalism has already **swallowed the framework**.  
They think we need to flip the script.  
But if you’re still holding the **same script**, you’re in the **same play** holding the script that they have right-side up, upside down.

You don’t dismantle white supremacy by painting it Black.  
You dismantle it by **leaving the stage** and building a new world **off-script**.

Supremacy is inherently **weak**.  
It’s for those who fear shared sunlight.  
It’s a worldview that assumes love is scarce, power is zero-sum, and belonging must be conquered.

Just being honest.

Blackness—at its deepest rhythm—**knows better.**  
Knows how to dance in exile.  
Knows how to feed multitudes from scraps.  
Knows how to *exist without erasure.*

**Why would we want to be “supreme” when we’ve always been sacred?**

We don’t want to be the new guys on top.

We need.

- Joy without surveillance.
- Truth without interruption.
- Memory without distortion.
- Futures that don’t require ritual resistance just to breathe.

We don’t want supremacy.  
We want the end of **supremacist architecture** altogether because it’s all built on illusions and fictitious sense of achievement.
